Chocolate Cake Recipe

The words “perfect” and “chocolate cake” basically have the same meaning, right? With homemade chocolate cake this seems to be true. Bake this delicious dessert when you’re hosting the next family get-together, celebrating a birthday or wanting a bite of delicious chocolate cake after dinner. This “perfectly chocolate” cake was made for sharing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 - 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cups coca powder
  • 1 - 1/2 tsps baking powder
  • 1 - 1/2 tsps baking soda
    • 1 tsps slat
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsps vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
